Our latest game is Resistance is Fruitile, an arena-style twin stick shooter:


Resistance is Fruitile

During the bride and groom's wedding celebration, a mysterious legion of fruit monsters suddenly materializes and steals their chocolate wedding cake!

Chocolate cake should NOT HAVE FRUIT INSIDE!!

Help the bride, with her powerful shotgun, and the groom, with his rapid-fire pistol, obliterate every single fruit monster that gets in their way!

Resistance is Fruitile is a challenging twin stick shooter featuring local co-op and a unique card perk system!
